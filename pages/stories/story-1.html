You find yourself in a clearing in the mountain top, the moon shining bright above you. Darkness surrounds you, when you hear a grim growl growing louder by the minute. Your hairs stand on end and your heart beats faster. Can this be... oh, it is.

A towering figure looks down at you menacingly. Its bright red eyes like two big stars of the night sky betraying its thirst for blood. Think fast: are you ready to take down the Ice Dragon by yourself with one of your spells or is it better to hide for now and come back later with some support?
